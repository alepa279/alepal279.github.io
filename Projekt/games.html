Coin Flip

Click to Play

Coin flip is a game where you flip a coin and bet on which side the coin is goint to land on. If your guess is correct you will win 10 point and if your guess is incorrect you will loose 10 points. It's a game about chance and with the right amount of luck you might leave with morethan what you came with. But most of the times you will just keep playing until your out of money because it's too fun to play.
